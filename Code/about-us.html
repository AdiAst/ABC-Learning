About Us

ABC Learning's vision is to become a leading technology education platform that encourages wider access to digital literacy for all. ABC Learning has a mission to accelerate Indonesia's transition to a digital world through technology education that transforms lives.

Now all nations are moving towards a digital world that relies on technological innovation in all aspects of life. We believe technology education is the foundation for every nation to be at the forefront of facing the digital world.

ABC Learning is here as a technology education platform that helps produce digital talent with global standards. All for the sake of accelerating Indonesia to be at the forefront.
